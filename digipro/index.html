WELCOME




SugarSweets is a family-owned gluten-free bakery and restaurant specializing in candy-flavored desserts. Our store offers delicious treats ranging from lollipop smoothies and sour sweet milkshakes, to chocolate sundaes, and much more. Besides our online store that will ship our gifts to anywhere in the US, SugarSweets can be found in Chicago, Los Angeles, Indianapolis,and New York City. We hope you you enjoy our desserts just as much as we do!
