ABOUT US

SugarSweets was created in 2018 by designer and chef, Kourtney Hedges. Kourtney's love for candy started when she was just a baby. Ever since she can remember, candy has been the highlight of her life. After graduating from art and culinary school, Kourtney started SugarSweets by selling her home-made candy desserts on online shops such as Etsy and Poshmark. Eventually, her products became so popular that she was able to open up her own buisness. SugarSweets has multiple bakeries open in Indianapolis, Indiana, Los Anegeles, California, New York City, New York, and Chicago, Illinois. We hope our candy desserts brings you happiness!
