About Us

"The best candy around"

Lollie's POPS is an organization that sells the best hard candy around town. With three locations in New York, California, and Indiana, Lollie's POPS sells over 1 billion pieces of hard candy each year. Ranging from delightful lolliepops to popping sour candy, Lollie's POPS has a range of yummy hard candy that would make any person's mouth water.
